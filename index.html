Crystals Collector

How to Play

You will be shown a random number at the start of the game. This is the number that you will need to match to win.

There are four crystals below that are worth a certain point value. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. By clicking on a crystal, you will add that specific amount of points to your total score.

To win the game, click on various crystals until you exactly match your total score to the random number generated at the beginning of the game. You Lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

Each time the game starts, it will generate new values for each of the crystals.
