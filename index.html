Crystals Collector

Instructions for How to Play

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. This is the number that you will need to match to win the game.

There are four crystals below that are worth a certain point value. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

To win the game you must exactly match your total score to the random number given at the beginning of the game. You lost the game if your total score goes above the random number.

Each time the game starts, it will generate new values for each of the crystals. These values are hidden from you until you click on a gem.
